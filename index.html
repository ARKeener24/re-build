Privacy Policy

Your resume stays yours.

Last updated · July 2026

In short: Re-Build keeps everything on your own computer. No account, no ads, no tracking — and nothing you enter is ever sent anywhere.

What we collect

Nothing. Re-Build does not collect, transmit, or share any personal information. There are no accounts, no advertising, and no analytics or tracking of any kind.

Where your data lives

Everything you enter — your resume content, saved resumes, templates, themes, and settings — is stored only on your own device, in your Documents\Re-Build folder. The app runs entirely offline; none of it reaches the developer or any third party.

Files you export

Resumes you export as Word (.docx) or PDF are saved to the location you choose on your device. They are never uploaded or copied anywhere else.

Uninstalling

Uninstalling Re-Build does not delete your saved data. You remain in control: to remove it, simply delete the Documents\Re-Build folder yourself.
